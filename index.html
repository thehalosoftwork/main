Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with HaloSoft

How long does a typical project take? +
Most websites are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. Mobile apps typically take 6-10 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.
What's included in the support? +
We include 3 months of post-launch support for fixes and minor updates. Additional support can be renewed as needed for continued assistance with your project.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! We understand cash flow is important for small businesses. We offer flexible payment plans with 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can discuss milestone-based payments.
Will I be able to update the website myself? +
Absolutely! We build all sites with user-friendly content management systems. We'll provide training and documentation so you can make updates whenever you need. Of course, we're always here if you prefer us to handle updates.
What makes HaloSoft different from other developers? +
We specialize in making professional development affordable for small businesses. While agencies charge $5,000-$20,000 for similar work, we offer competitive pricing without compromising quality. Plus, support is included—not an expensive add-on.
