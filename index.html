Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with HaloSoft.

How long does a typical project take? +
Most websites are completed within 2–4 weeks, depending on complexity. Mobile apps typically take 6–10 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated at every stage.
What's included in the post-launch support? +
All projects include 3 months of post-launch support — covering bug fixes, minor content updates, and guidance. After that, support can be renewed on a monthly basis.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes. We offer flexible payment plans with 50% upfront and 50% on completion. For larger projects, milestone-based payments can be arranged. We make it easy.
Will I be able to update the site myself? +
Absolutely. We build with user-friendly CMS tools and provide documentation and training so you can manage your content independently. Or we can handle updates for you.
What makes HaloSoft different from other developers? +
We specialize in making professional-quality development accessible for small businesses. Agencies typically charge $5,000–$20,000 for similar work. We deliver comparable results at a fraction of the cost — with support included, not upsold.
